Mission:

Ensuring the Safe Return Of Misplaced Items

Our mission is to provide a reliable and efficient lost and found management system that ensures the safe return of misplaced items to their rightful owners.

At The Lost and Found Information System, we are dedicated to providing a dependable and efficient service for handling lost and found items. We recognize the stress and inconvenience that come with losing personal belongings, which is why our mission is to streamline the process of reuniting people with their items. Our top priority is ensuring the safe return of misplaced items, whether it's a forgotten laptop, a set of keys, or a cherished accessory. With a commitment to prompt action and careful management, we strive to make the experience of retrieving lost items as smooth and stress-free as possible for all our users.

Vision:

Leading Lost and Found Solutions around Maseno University

Our vision is to become the leading provider of lost and found solutions on campuses nationwide, setting the standard for efficiency and effectiveness in managing lost and found items.

Our vision is to redefine the lost and found experience, transcending campus boundaries to create a seamless and efficient system that positively impacts communities across the Varsity. By setting the standard for lost and found management, we aspire to be the go-to solution for reuniting people with their lost items, making the process hassle-free and stress-free for all. Our commitment to efficiency and effectiveness drives us to continuously innovate and improve, ensuring that every lost item finds its way back to its rightful owner. Together, let's make lost and found a meaningful and impactful service that brings people together and fosters a sense of community care and support.

Core Values:

Integrity: We are committed to operating with honesty and transparency in all aspects of our business. Trust is essential in handling lost items, and we uphold the highest ethical standards.

Efficiency: We strive to provide quick and effective solutions for managing lost and found items. We understand the urgency of reuniting people with their belongings.

Customer Service: We prioritize customer satisfaction and go above and beyond to ensure a positive experience for all users of our system.

Innovation: We constantly seek new ways to improve and enhance our lost and found management system.

Team:

Our team consists of three dedicated professionals with expertise in technology, customer service, and lost and found management. Together, we are passionate about providing a solution that simplifies the process of retrieving misplaced items and ensures a positive experience for all users. Our team is composed of dedicated individuals who bring unique expertise and skills to ensure the success of our vision. Our technology expert is responsible for maintaining the technical aspects of our system, ensuring that it runs smoothly, securely handles data, and provides a user-friendly experience. The customer service specialist is focused on assisting users, addressing inquiries, guiding users through the process, and providing timely responses to ensure a positive experience for all. Lastly, our lost and found management professional oversees the entire lost and found process, managing reported items, tracking found items, and coordinating reunification efforts to ensure that every lost item finds its way back to its rightful owner. Together, our team is committed to redefining the lost and found experience and making a positive impact on communities across the Varsity.
