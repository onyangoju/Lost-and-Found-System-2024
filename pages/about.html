THE 123 OF LOST AND FOUND!

If you have lost an important item like your keys, laptop or any other valuable possession, the first thing you should do is report it to the Student Lost and Found Desk at the New Library. Additionally, please report it to us, as we maintain a real-time database of lost and found items. In case you find a lost item, please report it to us, and we will take care of the rest. Our goal is to reunite people with their lost items and make the process more efficient.

 

  1. To interact with us digitally and make the process smoother, you don't need an account. All you need is to click the Find Items to search for your lost item if it has been found yet or not. Only admins are allowed to log in to the system for security, this step was made specifically to make it easy and accept both the computer-literate and illiterate.
  2. If you’ve lost an important item, the initial step is to write an inquiry or message to us. There’s an avenue you can explore: Contact Us. We maintain a real-time database of lost and found items. By doing so, you increase the chances of reuniting with your lost belongings.
  3. On the flip side, if you happen to find a lost item, please Post Found Item. We’ll take care of the rest. Our mission is to reunite people with their lost possessions. By reporting found items, you contribute to this effort and help someone retrieve what’s rightfully theirs.
